Tom Evenwood's name appears on the guild's founding documents — Kisara insisted on it, saying she had met him once, on the outer road, and that he was the kind of person you made room for.

He was not at the Last Sound Inn that evening. Where he was is a question the others have not yet had occasion to ask.

His history will be written as it is discovered.
