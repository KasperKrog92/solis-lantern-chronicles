Tom is a craftsman and semi-retired mentor in his late 40s with a long career behind him. He has spent decades teaching magical engineering and working with adventuring parties and military outfits, building and maintaining the kind of gear that keeps people alive in places where things go wrong. He is not a soldier and has never pretended to be, but he understands what soldiers need, and that has made him quietly indispensable to more than a few people over the years.

Earlier in his career, he worked as a researcher in the Inner Tier on a secret government project studying the effects of Leviathan blood on living subjects. The project involved illegal experimentation. Tom refused to experiment on others, but had no such reservations about using himself — he injected himself with refined Leviathan blood on multiple occasions, and developed, as a result, enhanced cognitive abilities and an unusually precise memory that he has never fully explained to anyone. When the project was eventually exposed and shut down, Tom was removed from his position and banished to the Middle Tier. The official action was carried out by Dolores Hylach, then as now the Minister of Empire Integrity. He believes someone on the research team informed on him, though he has never known who. Every appeal he made afterward was quietly buried.

He spent the years since teaching magical engineering in the Middle Tier, where Fentley Royce became one of his apprentices — bright, obsessive, better with machines than with people, the kind of student you remember. When Fentley's letter arrived, the urgency in it was unmistakable: *a magical armor, of the utmost importance, come as soon as you can*. He didn't deliberate long. He arrived in Brinegate travelling against the flow of people moving inland ahead of the wet season, passed people on the road who looked at him like he was going the wrong direction, and came anyway.

He knows Kisara Solis from past work — her guild's gear has passed through his hands before — and through her he has found his way to the Solis Lantern. What he found at Fentley's workshop is his introduction to something much larger than a commission. He has no idea what he has walked into. Not yet.

He has a mechanical mage hand named Arthur.
