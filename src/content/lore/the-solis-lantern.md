The Solis Lantern was founded informally on the night of the graveyard test, and formally in the following week when Kisara registered the name with Brinegate's guild authority — a process that required three pieces of documentation, a membership list of at least four active members, and a small fee that Nyssara paid with coins she declined to account for.

The name comes from two sources: Kisara's family name, and the image of a lantern that she carried as a personal emblem throughout her years in the Outer Tier. The lantern, she said at the founding, is what you hold up when you are going into a dark place. Not because it makes the dark less dark, but because it lets you see what you're walking toward.

## Charter

The guild operates primarily in the border districts of Brinegate and the accessible portions of the Outer Tier. Its stated purpose is to address problems that fall into the gap between what the garrison is mandated to handle and what ordinary citizens can manage themselves — the vines along the Wall, the things that come through the lower gates at the wrong time, the missing persons reports that the official records file under *outer road casualties* and decline to investigate.

The guild takes on contracted work. It also takes on work that no one has contracted, when the judgment of its members determines that the work needs to be done.

## Current Members

Kisara Solis, founder and nominal leader. She describes her own role as *the person who knows where the guild hall keys are kept*, which is a deflection that the others find characteristic.

Mara Embervale, Wynn, Nyssara, and Querc — the founding party.

Tom Evenwood, listed on the charter at Kisara's insistence.

The guild is not currently accepting additional members. This may change.
