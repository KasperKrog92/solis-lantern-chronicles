---
title: "The Lantern Accords"
category: "History"
---

Following the Sundering Wars, the seven city-states of the interior agreed to a set of trade and non-aggression treaties known as the Lantern Accords. The name comes from the ritual of lighting a single lantern at the signing table — a symbol that one flame can hold back the dark if tended by many hands.

The Accords are overseen by the Conclave of Wicks, a council of neutral arbiters who convene in the city of Solis once per year. Their authority is largely ceremonial, but their symbolic weight has kept the peace for three generations.
