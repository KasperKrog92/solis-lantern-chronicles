The session opened with three strangers sharing a table at the Lantern's Rest. A merchant named Harlow approached them with a nervous proposition: his regular guards had vanished, and the caravan needed to leave by dawn.

On the road, the party encountered a collapsed bridge and was forced to negotiate passage through the Ashfen marsh — where something watched them from beneath the water.
