Otto Einfasen holds a bureaucratic position in Brinegate under the Ministry of Empire Integrity, the ministry responsible for internal threats to Imperial stability. He is, officially, a local contact for the Minister — a person of use to Nyssara should she require information or help navigating the city's more complicated processes.

Dolores recommended him in her letters. *Capable and discreet*, she said. Nyssara has made little use of him so far.

He is a Drow man in his thirties, polished and measured in the way of people who are careful about what they reveal. He is not difficult to find. He is not particularly forthcoming. He gives the impression of someone who considers every answer before offering it — which is either the mark of a careful professional or of someone with a great deal to be careful about.

Whether he is exactly what Dolores believes him to be is a question Nyssara has not yet had occasion to investigate.
