Elody Embervale is a tiefling scientist in her late twenties, red-skinned like her mother and her sister. She was completing her studies in Leviathan Biology at a university in the Inner Tier when Alicent stopped answering letters. She did not wait long before deciding to act.

Her last letter to Mara said only that she was on her way to Brinegate and would be arriving shortly. She named no specific date. She asked Mara not to worry. She never arrived.

Mara has been watching the gate ever since — scanning the flow of people coming through from the Outer Tier, looking for a red-skinned tiefling with dark hair who moves like someone who knows where she is going. She has not found her yet.

Whether Elody made it to Brinegate and then vanished, or never made it at all, is a question the party has not yet been able to answer.
