Tristan Bas is a human linguist and anthropologist in his early thirties. Warm and intellectually curious, he spent several months living with Querc's indigenous tribe, learning their language and documenting their oral traditions with the kind of focused attention that comes from believing the work genuinely matters. He befriended Querc during that time, told them about the wider world, and left a connection between them that neither quite finished.

He returned to his research before anything was resolved. He mentioned, when he left, that he would be stationed at a facility called Jakata in the Outer Tier — close to Brinegate, he said. Close enough.

Querc has been in Brinegate for three months. Jakata is on the other side of the Wall, and the Wall requires papers that haven't come through yet. Tristan has not written. Whether that means something has happened to him, or simply that he didn't know where to send a letter, is a question the Outer Tier hasn't answered yet.
