Fentley Royce has a reputation in the craftsman quarter for being intensely focused, kind-natured, and sometimes so absorbed in his work that he misses what is happening around him. He trained under Tom Evenwood for several years and never quite lost the habit of reaching back to his old mentor when things got complicated — which makes the letter he sent, urgent and carefully underplayed, entirely in character.

The letter described a piece of magical armor he was working on. *Of the utmost importance*, he wrote. *Come as soon as you can.* He gave no further details. Tom came.

Fentley's workshop in the craftsman quarter was locked for weeks before anyone investigated. His brother Thorvald noticed first — not a man who makes social calls, but a man who pays attention. The city guard, occupied with wet season preparations, declined to investigate. The Solis Lantern did not.

What they found was a workshop that had been searched thoroughly and professionally. Fentley himself was gone, and the circumstances of his departure did not suggest he left by choice.
