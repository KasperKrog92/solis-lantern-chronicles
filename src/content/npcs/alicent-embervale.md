Alicent Embervale is a tiefling scientist in her early sixties who has spent most of her career studying Leviathan biology in the Outer Tier. By all accounts she was brilliant, driven, and deeply committed to her research — the kind of scientist for whom the work was never merely professional. She reached the rank of Head Researcher at the Jakata facility after decades of work that her colleagues described as relentless.

Several months ago, her letters to her daughter Mara stopped arriving. No explanation. No warning. Just silence. When Mara's older sister Elody went to investigate, she too went silent. Elody's last letter placed her en route to Brinegate. She never arrived.

The research facility at Jakata has no record, officially, of any irregularity. The gate authorities have no record of Alicent leaving the Outer Tier. No one in a position to give answers has been willing to give them.

What Alicent was working on in the months before her disappearance — and why it might have made her disappear — is a question the party has only begun to ask.
