Kisara Solis operated in the Outer Tier for over a decade, mostly in the Wall-adjacent districts where the Leviathan blood concentration is high enough to make ordinary patrols unreliable. She developed a reputation there for being, in equal measure, effective and difficult to categorize, the kind of person other experienced adventurers spoke of with a particular combination of respect and the unease that tends to attach to someone who has seen too much and processed it all.
