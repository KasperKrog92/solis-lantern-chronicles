Kisara Solis operated in the Outer Tier for over a decade, mostly in the Wall-adjacent districts where the Leviathan blood concentration is high enough to make ordinary patrols unreliable. She developed a reputation there for being, in equal measure, effective and difficult to categorize — the kind of person other experienced adventurers spoke of with a particular combination of respect and the unease that tends to attach to someone who has seen too much and processed it all.

She retired from active work two years before the founding of the guild. She did not return to the inner districts, as most people do when they retire. She came to Brinegate. She visited the graveyard east of the outer gate with a regularity that the gate guards noted and did not discuss.

When she decided to establish the guild, she did not advertise. She watched. She waited for the particular combination of capability and character that she had in mind, and when she found it — at a table near the hearth at the Last Sound Inn — she moved quickly.

The test was, as she admitted at the graveyard, a confession as much as an evaluation. She needed to know if the people she was trusting with something important were trustworthy. She also needed, it turned out, to be trusted by someone else.

She keeps the guild's books. She handles the gate authority registration and the garrison liaison work that the others find tedious. She does not go on missions herself, but she knows every route between the inner and outer Wall districts, and her knowledge of which people in Brinegate can be relied upon is considerable.
